Syracuse University, Fall 2025

Looking ahead, the Bronx will see more than 1,000 tree plantings in the December 2025 season, an almost 50% increase from the previous planting season. In the January 2026 planting season, there are no tree plantings planned for the Manhatten or Staten Island boroughs, but over 2,500 for Queens. Of the six tree plantings planned for Manhatten in the June 2026 planting season, four are in city council district six.
