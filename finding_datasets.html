Link: NHL draft data since 1963

  1. How is this data organized and collected? The data is organized based on draft order in each draft year, earliest 1963 and latest 2022. It is collected from publicly available draft statistics.
  2. Why is this newsworthy? Who would be a good audience for a story on this? It is newsworthy based on how the National Hockey League has changed over time and how more immigrants have come from around the world to contribute to the league. This is a hot button issue with how recent policy has targeted immigration. The best audience would be hockey fans and people interested in immigration issues.
  3. What is one data hypothesis you could make about this dataset? The majority of NHL draftees are from Canada and the US, but as time has gone on since 1963, more draftees have come from a number of different countries and continue to be drafted in higher and higher positions, compared to lower draft positions in years past.
